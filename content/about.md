Hi there, I'm Lei Zhang. 👋

I'm a Ph.D. student at the **University of Chinese Academy of Sciences**, Beijing, China, with research interests in **Agentic Coding**, **Agentic Infra**, and **Agentic Reinforcement Learning**.

I recently completed an internship at **Alibaba Qwen Group** (Aug. 2024 – Mar. 2026), where I was responsible for code evaluation pipelines for the **Qwen2.5 and Qwen2.5-Coder** series, and for the agentic coding infrastructure and training data synthesis for the **Qwen3 and Qwen3-Coder** series.

I believe that almost everything in the modern world is built on software — and software is built on code. A model that truly understands and writes code is not just a tool for developers; it is foundational to nearly every future application of AI. This conviction drives my focus on advancing model capabilities in **agentic coding** scenarios, where I see the greatest potential for real-world impact.
